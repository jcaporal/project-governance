SHIFT

Strategic HR Implementation for Focused Transformation

1. Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive evaluation of HRIS options for our organization: Sprout HR + Payroll (Baseline), BambooHR (Alternative), Workday Human Capital Management (HCM) + Timekeeping + Payroll (Recommended), and Workday Phased Approach (HCM + Timekeeping).

  • Sprout: Current state with no improvements; highly manual, fragmented data, and poor reporting affecting BI, WFM, and Data Science teams.
  • BambooHR: Sample proposal received (potentially negotiable). Covers HR and timekeeping, but payroll and benefits administration are available as US-only add-ons. Free trial available for validation.
  • Workday: Cost estimates based on publicly available information. Most requirements appear supported; PH payroll compliance confirmation pending. Offers unified HCM, Time, and Payroll solution.

2. Current State: Sprout Baseline

  • Manual HR and Payroll processes across HRIS and BI teams.
  • Inefficient reporting; WFM and Data Science involved in data cleaning and reconciliation.
  • Limited scalability and compliance coverage.
  • Annual Cost: ~$96,307 (HR + Payroll + Web Bundy).
